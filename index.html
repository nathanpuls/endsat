What is ends.at?

It's a simple, shortlink tool connected to billions of websites. In the examples below ,"*" represents your keyword or search phrase.

ends.at/* goes to DuckDuckGo's first search result. E.g. taylorswift.comends.at/taylorswiftSometimes typos even work.ends.at/taylordwift

*.ends.at/* piggy backs on DuckDuckGo's !Bang search toolwhich has over 13,000 websites connected. yt.ends.at goes to YouTube.com yt.ends.at/pickleball searches pickleball on YouTube

The sky's the limit.

When typing or sharing an ends.at link just remember that spaces are not allowed. Put a "." or "+" between words or topics.

The link destination may change if the search result is no longer the first result on DuckDuckGo.

Previewing links before sharing to see where they go is not a bad idea.
