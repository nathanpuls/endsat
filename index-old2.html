ends.at

A simple search and shortlink tool connected to over 13,000 websites.

It piggy backs on DuckDuckGo's Bangs. Bangs are shortcuts that quickly take you to search results on other sites.

See all the available bangs

Examples:

[website].ends.at/[search] yt.ends.at goes to YouTube.com yt.ends.at/pickleball searches pickleball on YouTube
